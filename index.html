Approach

We pair design instinct with data. Popular-times analysis, seat-mix studies and dwell-time modelling that make a space perform as well as it photographs.

Sociable Weavers is a hospitality and F&B interior architecture studio based in Bengaluru, led by a designer with over a decade shaping spaces at Gensler and beyond.

We work across resorts, restaurants and private residences, from concept and master planning through detailed GFC drawings and on-site coordination.
